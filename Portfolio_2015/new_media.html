Monsters and Ladders

A spin-off of the board game, "Shoots and Ladders". If the game was real, this is what the design layout out would look like for the game's main, options, and play states. The goal of the game is to climb up as many ladders to earn points until the monster or nightmare catches up with the player. Made in fall of 2015.

The image on the right is the responsive design for the website if the viewer was looking at the website on a mobile device.
