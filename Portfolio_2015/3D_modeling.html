3D Modeling

Bamboo Spigot

My Bamboo Spigot model mainly focused on several animations at once. You have the faucet handle turning that releases the marble from the nose of the fountain. This marble represents "water" that would fill the spigot. The spigot would then tip over to empty itself out. The primary focus of this model was to capture an accurate motion of the spigot tipping over and returning to its original state. Made in fall 2015.

Bunker Model

The Bunker was inspired by the bunker in Star Wars. This is a model that I created in Maya that would be later used in a Unity group project. There is a lot less details in this bunker than the one from the movie and it took me several of tries to model the Bunker the way I had envisioned. I used textures and skinned animation onto the model. Made in fall of 2015.
